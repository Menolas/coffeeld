Our Story

Coffeeld was founded with an aim to become the best coffee shop in New York City.

We began cooperating with the best coffee vendors and purchasing the finest ingredients to prepare delicious coffee for our customers. We have created a cozy home-like atmosphere at our shops so that our visitors could feel relaxed and carefree.

Our bakery, cookies, and sweets are made by professional pastry chefs whose only aim is to make delightful freshly-baked goods for our customers.
